About Me

Hello, I'm Sasha

I'm a 100% self-taught programmer and have been programming in various languages for 2+ years. I have extensive experience in web development and basic knowledge of game development concepts.

I signed up for Digital Art because I've always had creative visions, but never had the skills or the know-how to make those visions come to life. Both Photoshop and Illustrator are far superior to any software I have ever used before, and I heavily use them both in and out of class for both school and personal projects.

I assumed the class would be very illustration-focused, but I misjudged how Illustrator-focused it would be. Nevertheless, I'm very thankful I was given the time and resources needed to produce my best work.

Despite my experience in Premiere, I still found the class challenging because I ultimately decided to use After Effects along with Premiere for my video project. Contrary to what I assumed, my background in Premiere did not translate to After Effects, leading me to many hours of learning the ins-and-outs of After Effects. Even though I barely had enough time to finish my project and had to cut a fair bit out, I still feel very proud of not just the work, but also the skills I learned along the way.

When I first stepped foot into the class, I wanted to create something I could feel proud of. Throughout the course, I surprised myself with my ability to learn something in one program and apply those same concepts in another program, along with how I could pick up a program for the first time and immediately make something cool.
